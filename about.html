Welcome To My Website

My name is Fatima Nasr. I am originally from Syria and due to the political situation, I had to relocate to Kurdistan where I am currently living with my family. As every new beginning, it was quite difficult to adapt.

Leaving my home and my friends was a challenge, however I managed to overcome these difficulties and have found friends pretty quickly.

In terms of living situation, my family and I are now living inside a camp. Again, something we all had to get used to.

My family and I are close, which is great,so we managed to turn this situation into something positive.

During this time, I worked with the organization “Save the Children” which I found tremendously inspiring.

After that, I also completed an apprenticeship with a hotel, where I was able to use my skill-set to deal with different people and learn how to work under pressure.

This enabled me to improve my English language skills.

Whilst I had to overcome a number of challenging situations,it also helped me in many ways.

I am very grateful that I can continue studying in Kurdistan and can focus on learning and developing myself further.

I heard about Re:coded from an organization inside the refugee camp and was interested immediately.

Re:coded was an experience I value highly. It’s a great thing to learn the English language with programming language at the same time.

I am someone who loves learning and discovering new things.When I started learning coding I was so happy because it’s so useful and professional.

Upon completion, I hope to utilize my skills and add value to a great team and company. After Recoded, I plan to continue learning programming and new coding languages.

There were professional and amazing teachers for English and programming languages.

My English language before Re:coded was average, but after I joined I became able to speak well in front of anyone. It was a really amazing opportunity for me.

I hope to study programming at the local university. I want develop new technology because technology is the most important thing in our world.
